Our Story

Driven By A Passion
For The Craft

CSFT was not born from a desire to process numbers. It was forged from a deep-rooted passion for true tactical capability and student-first education.

Frans van der Walt established Counter Strike Firearms Training to elevate the standard of firearm competency. Recognizing a gap in the industry where minimal compliance often replaced actual skill, Frans built an academy dedicated to genuine proficiency.

Here, the student's operational survival and confidence take absolute precedence over mere certification.

Mission Directive

Empowering Through
Absolute Competence

To equip individuals with uncompromising practical skills, critical legal knowledge, and high-level tactical competence, ensuring they can handle firearms safely, legally, and lethally when required.

Target Audiences

01. Private Individuals

Citizens seeking personal self-defense mastery, sport shooting excellence, or foundational firearm confidence.

02. Security Professionals

Active personnel requiring mandatory PSIRA certifications, renewals, and advanced tactical response training.

03. Tactical Career Seekers

Driven individuals starting from basic Grade E, aiming to ascend to elite specialized security management roles.

04. Security Firms

Corporations requiring standardized, ruthless excellence and compliance training for their operational squads.

05. Range Safety Aspirants

Veterans aiming to become certified range officers and instructors, upholding the highest echelons of safety protocols.

The CSFT
Philosophy

"At Most Respect"

Our core value is unshakeable. Every individual who steps onto our range—whether a nervous civilian novice or a hardened tactical veteran—is treated with absolute, uncompromising respect.

We deliver high-level customer service intertwined with elite instruction. We don't just process students; we cultivate protectors in an environment of dignity and rigorous discipline.
